About us
The Smile Store Dental Clinic welcomes you as a patient. We Use latest technology to offer excellent dental care to provide a pleasant stress free experience. Our goal is to help our patient achieve optimal health care and to create beautiful smile. Being a multi-speciality dental clinic, our mavens span across different areas of dentistry right from endodontists to implant experts, paediatric dentistry (pedodontist), orthodontists, orofacial pain specialists, as well as smile designers and dental s urgeons. Whether it is a smile correction treatment or root canal treatment, tooth whitening, and much more, we provide you with the right action for all your dental problems.

We are one of the Best dental clinic with a team of well qualified and highly experienced team of dentist and surgeons. We incorporate skill, experience and technology under one roof. The work of our highly skilled doctors of varying disciplines of dentistry, coupled with the use of the latest equipment and technologies ensures that each patient is treated with the highest standard. Our complete team functions with sole purpose of ensuring complete patient care and satisfaction, and this dedication is what always set us apart from our counterparts. We take special care of the infection control and have a strict protocol for sterilization to ensure impeccable service to all of our patients.

Meet the Duo


Dr. Trapti & Dr. Sachin are a dynamic duo in both their personal and professional spheres of life and share common core values of bringing compassion and passion to their day to day work. “Every patient is special & dear to us and we value the trust that has been placed upon us” is the tenant on which they have built The Smile Dental Clinic and have ensured that this core value is inculcated into the entire team of Doctors and Staff.


Meet Our Team
Dr. Trapti Sharma Thakur MDS, Endodontist (Root Canal Specialist)
Dr. Trapti Sharma Thakur has received her Master's Degree in the field of Root Canal Therapy and Operative Dentistry. She specializes in root canal therapy, smile design and is Meticulous by nature, she incorporates this quality into her work and continually works towards updating her knowledge, skills and equipment needed to provide her patients with a painless and stress-free experience.
Her extensive experience in CADCAM dental technology, gives her a synergistic advantage in aesthetic and restorative dentistry. She is an active academician and conducts various courses to impart knowledge to the budding doctors.

Dr. Sachin Thakur MDS, Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon
Dr. Sachin Thakur is an acclaimed oral and facio-maxillary surgeon from Indore. He has graduated from College of Dental Sciences, Indore and completed his masters in Oral & Maxillofacial surgery from DAVV University, Indore.
He has been an avid speaker on Oral Implantology and Facial deformity corrections in various CDE and CME across Central India and has conducted many training programs on Oral Implantology.He has been counted under intriguing, ingenious and innovative surgeons of Indore who has been exclusively practicing Facial corrective surgery and Implantology.

Dr. Nivedita Jain MDS, Pedodontist (Child Specialist)
Dr. Nivedita Jain (Pedodontist) is known for her clinical acumen and her patient centric approach. Not only does she provide technically exceptional treatment to her beautiful innocent child patients, her ability to put them at ease on the dental chair allows for a comforting doctor-patient relationship; which is of utmost importance in the field of Pediatric dentistry and works towards preventive dental care and instills a positive attitude towards dental health in children.
